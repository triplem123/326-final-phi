About Roomio

  • Roomio is your go to tool to help you visualize your rooms
  • Use our tools to put your ideas in action
  • Unlock added features by creating an account!

Loading Templates:

  • Login to your account and head to MyRooms to select from a variety of presets
  • Or create your own room

Customize your room:

  • Resize your room by dragging walls by the corners
  • Double click the wall to add a new corner
  • Right click on the corner to get rid of it
  • Click on the pieces of furniture on the left to customize your room
  • Resize by pulling from the bottom right or delete the piece by a right click!
